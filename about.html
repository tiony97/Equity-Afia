About Equity Afia

Our Approach

Equity Afia is a sustainable, integrated health model that is using franchising principles to train and monitor a network of Medical-Entrepreneurs to provide standardized, quality and affordable healthcare services throughout Kenya. Equity Afia plans to provide comprehensive outpatient services through an estimated 300 outpatient health facilities over the next few years. Our main goal is to ensure sustained improvement of the health and well-being of Kenyans by increasing access to and utilization of quality, affordable and standardized healthcare

Prof. Isaac Macharia

Executive Chairman

PROF. ISAAC MACHARIA

NON-EXECUTIVE CHAIRMAN

Professor Isaac Macharia is a distinguished academic and business leader with extensive experience in corporate governance. He has served in various leadership positions in both public and private institutions.

With a background in medicine and business administration, Prof. Macharia brings a unique perspective to the boardroom. He has been instrumental in guiding the organization's strategic direction and ensuring strong corporate governance practices.

Prof. Macharia has received numerous accolades for his contributions to both the academic and business communities in Kenya and across East Africa.

Dr. James Mwangi, CBS

Group Managing Director

DR. JAMES MWANGI, CBS

GROUP MANAGING DIRECTOR AND CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

James holds five honorary doctorate degrees in recognition of his positive impact on the Kenyan society. He holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree and is a Certified Public Accountant. He has been honored thrice with National Presidential Awards: The First Class Chief of the Order of the Burning Spear (CBS), the Moran of the Burning Spear (MBS) and Head of State Commendation (HSC).

On the global front, he has won several awards including the G8 Global Vision Award, 2007. He was named among the Top 50 Emerging Market Business Leaders and the 20 most influential people in Africa in 2011; the World Entrepreneur of the Year by Ernst & Young in 2012; the Forbes Africa Person of the Year in 2012 and was recently named in the Bloomberg 50 list of people who defined 2019 globally. He is an honoree of the 2020 Oslo Business for Peace Award, the Nobel Prize for business.

Jonas Mushosho

Non-Executive Director

JONAS MUSHOSHO

NON-EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Jonas Mushosho brings extensive experience in corporate governance and strategic leadership to the board. With a background in finance and business management, he has served on numerous boards across various industries.

His expertise in risk management and corporate strategy has been invaluable in guiding the organization through periods of growth and transformation. Mr. Mushosho is known for his keen analytical skills and ability to identify emerging market trends.

Prior to his current role, he held senior executive positions in several multinational corporations, where he was responsible for driving operational excellence and sustainable growth.

Dr. Evans Baiya

Non-Executive Director

DR. EVANS BAIYA

NON-EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Dr. Evans Baiya is a technology and innovation expert with a distinguished career spanning both academia and industry. He holds a PhD in Electrical Engineering and has been at the forefront of technological innovation in Africa.

His expertise in digital transformation and emerging technologies has been instrumental in guiding the organization's technology strategy. Dr. Baiya is passionate about leveraging technology to drive financial inclusion and economic development.

In addition to his board responsibilities, Dr. Baiya is a sought-after speaker and advisor on technology and innovation matters, and has published extensively in his field.
